Text — Questions 28–30
📄 Reading Passage
There are a lot of discussions as to whether children should be given homework or not. Some people claim that children do enough work in school already. They also argue that children have hobbies that they want to do after school, such as sports or music. A further point they make is that a lot of homework is pointless and doesn't help the child learn at all. However, there are also strong arguments against this point of view. Parents and teachers argue that it is important to find out whether children can work on their own without the support from the teacher. They say that the evening is a good time for children to sit down and think about what they have learned in school. Furthermore they claim that the school day is too short to get anything done that needs doing and it makes sense to send home tasks like independent reading or further writing tasks that don't need teacher's support. On balance, I think that some homework is a good idea but that it should only be given at the weekend when children have more time.

II. Essay
38
Write down the types of letter!
JAWABAN
Ada 2 jenis surat:
  • Formal Letter (Business/Official Letter) — surat resmi, contoh: surat lamaran kerja, surat pengaduan.
  • Informal Letter (Personal Letter) — surat tidak resmi, contoh: surat kepada teman atau keluarga.
39
How many types of Personal Letter? What are they?
JAWABAN
Ada 2 jenis Personal Letter:
  • Formal Personal Letter — surat pribadi yang bersifat resmi, contoh: application letter (surat lamaran), complaint letter.
  • Informal Personal Letter — surat pribadi tidak resmi, contoh: surat kepada teman, surat kepada keluarga.
40
Write down the generic structure of Formal Personal Letter!
JAWABAN
  • 1. Sender's Address (alamat pengirim)
  • 2. Date (tanggal)
  • 3. Inside Address / Recipient's Address (alamat penerima)
  • 4. Salutation (salam pembuka)
  • 5. Body of the Letter (isi surat: opening paragraph, content, closing paragraph)
  • 6. Complimentary Close (salam penutup)
  • 7. Signature & Name (tanda tangan dan nama)
41
Write down some examples of Salutation!
JAWABAN
  • Dear Sir,
  • Dear Madam,
  • Dear Mr. [Last Name],
  • Dear Mrs. [Last Name],
  • To Whom It May Concern,
42
Write down some examples of Complimentary Close!
JAWABAN
  • Yours sincerely, (digunakan bila nama penerima diketahui)
  • Yours faithfully, (digunakan bila nama penerima tidak diketahui)
  • Best regards,
  • Warm regards,
43
Write down some styles of letter!
JAWABAN
  • Full Block Style — semua bagian surat rata kiri.
  • Modified Block Style — tanggal, salam penutup, dan tanda tangan di tengah/kanan.
  • Semi-Block Style — seperti modified block, tetapi paragraf diindentasi.
  • Indented Style — setiap awal paragraf diindentasi ke dalam.
44
What is the content of Inside Address?
JAWABAN
Inside Address berisi:
  • Nama lengkap penerima
  • Jabatan/posisi penerima
  • Nama perusahaan/instansi
  • Alamat lengkap penerima (jalan, kota, kode pos)
45
Write the generic structure of Discussion Text!
JAWABAN
  • 1. Issue — permasalahan yang akan dibahas.
  • 2. Arguments For — argumen / pendapat yang mendukung.
  • 3. Arguments Against — argumen / pendapat yang menentang.
  • 4. Conclusion / Recommendation — kesimpulan atau saran penulis.
46
How many types of Exposition Text? What are they?
JAWABAN
Ada 2 jenis Exposition Text:
  • Analytical Exposition Text — meyakinkan pembaca dengan argumentasi; diakhiri dengan Reiteration (penegasan ulang).
  • Hortatory Exposition Text — mengajak pembaca untuk melakukan sesuatu; diakhiri dengan Recommendation (saran/anjuran).
47
Write the generic structure of Hortatory Exposition Text!
JAWABAN
  • 1. Thesis — pernyataan posisi / topik yang akan dibahas.
  • 2. Arguments — argumen-argumen yang mendukung thesis.
  • 3. Recommendation — saran/anjuran kepada pembaca untuk bertindak.
48
Write the generic structure of Analytical Exposition Text!
JAWABAN
  • 1. Thesis — pernyataan posisi / topik yang akan dibahas.
  • 2. Arguments — argumen-argumen yang mendukung thesis.
  • 3. Reiteration — penegasan ulang / kesimpulan dari thesis.
49
Write down the expression of opening paragraph in Application Letter!
JAWABAN

Write down the expression of closing paragraph in Application Letter!
