I. Multiple Choice
1
Anita : What's wrong with me? I have a terrible headache.
Brian : ……….. Have you taken any aspirin?
Anita : Yes, I took one an hour ago, but the pain hasn't gone.
A. After that, you need to lay down
B. If I were you, I wouldn't take the aspirin
C. You'd better lay down for a while ✔
D. The doctor has come yet
E. Don't sigh
JAWABAN C — Brian memberikan saran kepada Anita yang sakit kepala agar beristirahat.
2
Which of the following is an expression of Offering Help?
A. I am sorry to hear that.
B. I don't think so.
C. Would you like some help? ✔
D. Can you do me a favor?
E. Thank you.
JAWABAN C — "Would you like some help?" adalah ekspresi menawarkan bantuan.
3
"Can I help you carry your groceries?"
The expression above is ……….
A. Offering Goods or Services
B. Offering Assistance ✔
C. Offering Emotional Support
D. Offering Skills or Knowledge
E. Offering Time
JAWABAN B — Menawarkan untuk membantu membawakan belanjaan = Offering Assistance.
4
"I have some books you can borrow if you need them."
The expression above is ……….
A. Offering Emotional Support
B. Offering Skills or Knowledge
C. Offering Time
D. Offering Sharing ✔
E. Offering Assistance
JAWABAN D — Menawarkan buku untuk dipinjam = Offering Sharing (berbagi barang).
5
"If you need someone to talk to or listen, I'm here for you."
The expression above is ……….
A. Offering Emotional Support ✔
B. Offering Skills or Knowledge
C. Offering Time
D. Offering Sharing
E. Offering Assistance
JAWABAN A — "I'm here for you" = menawarkan dukungan emosional.
6
"Would you like a cup of coffee?"
The expression above is ……….
A. Offering Goods or Services ✔
B. Offering Assistance
C. Offering Emotional Support
D. Offering Skills or Knowledge
E. Offering Time
JAWABAN A — Menawarkan minuman (barang) = Offering Goods or Services.
7
"I know a lot about cooking. If you need a recipe or advice, just ask."
The expression above is ……….
A. Offering Goods or Services
B. Offering Assistance
C. Offering Emotional Support
D. Offering Skills or Knowledge ✔
E. Offering Time
JAWABAN D — Menawarkan keahlian memasak = Offering Skills or Knowledge.
8
Aldo : "What should I do to make a better result in the next test?"
Ryo : "………..."
A. I think you should learn more diligently ✔
B. You should quit from your school
C. There is no way you can make a better result
D. I think you're not a smart student
E. If I were you, I would not worry it
JAWABAN A — Saran yang paling tepat dan positif untuk meningkatkan hasil ujian.
9
Arjuna : I think I have a stomachache. It really hurts…..
Shinta : You should take medicine and you can buy it at the chemist.
Arjuna's best response is ………
A. That's a good idea! ✔
B. What's your feeling?
C. Tell anyone about it!
D. Do you have any idea?
E. You can do anything
JAWABAN A — Arjuna menerima saran Shinta dengan positif.
10
Arya : I'm very tired of having non-stop walking for an hour. I feel thirsty.
Doni : ………..
Arya : That's very kind of you.
A. Can you give me a bottle of milk?
B. Would you like some mineral water? ✔
C. Would you like to have some cakes?
D. Do you want to have some cake?
E. I don't have mineral water
JAWABAN B — Arya haus, Doni menawarkan air minum. Arya berterima kasih → tawaran diterima.
11
Tiwi : It's so cool here.
Tika : ..... and make hot tea for you?
A. Would you like me to turn on the AC
B. Would you like to go out for a while
C. Would you like me to turn on the heater ✔
D. Would you like to use our new fan
E. Would you like something to eat
JAWABAN C — Tiwi kedinginan ("cool"), Tika menawarkan untuk menyalakan pemanas dan membuat teh hangat.
12
Alex : The box you brought looks very heavy. Would you like me to help you?
Anton : ………………. I can lift it by myself.
A. Sure
B. I'm sorry, I can't
C. Certainly
D. No thanks ✔
E. Yes, please! Thank you.
JAWABAN D — Anton menolak bantuan dengan sopan karena bisa mengangkat sendiri.
